Dar Darkoum

Dar is a charming Moroccan restaurant nestled in the heart of Italy, offering a delightful fusion of Italian warmth and Moroccan hospitality. Located in a quaint corner of Florence, Dar transports its patrons to the vibrant streets of Marrakech with its ornate decor and tantalizing aromas.

As you step through the intricately carved wooden door, you are greeted by the rich scent of spices and the sound of soft Moroccan music playing in the background. The interior is adorned with colorful mosaic tiles, plush cushions, and intricate lanterns, creating an atmosphere that is both exotic and inviting.

The menu at Dar features a tantalizing array of traditional Moroccan dishes, prepared with authentic ingredients and spices imported directly from Morocco. From fragrant tagines bursting with tender meats and vegetables to fluffy couscous dishes topped with savory sauces, each bite is a culinary adventure through the streets of Marrakech.

Guests can savor classic dishes like lamb tagine with apricots and almonds or indulge in specialties like chicken pastilla, a delicate pastry filled with spiced meat and topped with powdered sugar and cinnamon. Vegetarian options abound, with flavorful dishes like vegetable couscous and stuffed bell peppers appealing to non-meat eaters.

To complement the meal, Dar offers a selection of Moroccan teas and refreshing beverages, including mint tea served in traditional glasses and freshly squeezed fruit juices.

With its warm ambiance, delectable cuisine, and attentive service, Dar is not just a restaurant but a cultural experience, inviting guests to savor the flavors of Morocco in the heart of Italy.

Join us at Dar for a culinary journey to Marrakech, right here in Florence.
